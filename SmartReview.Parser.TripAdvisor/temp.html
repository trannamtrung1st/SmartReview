      About
      Good food, good mood! At our restaurant, you’ll find fresh, hearty dishes that take advantage of the food paradise that is Ho Chi Minh City. This is the perfect place to get the party started, have a full-blown meal, or a nightcap before bed. Conveniently located in Bui Vien Street, The Garlik is right where the world comes to party in Saigon! Mix and mingle with friends, colleagues and business partners in our restaurant for some smashing local cuisine. Our menu combines the hearty southern way of cooking and the modern beat of the city, the result is contemporary Vietnamese cuisine that tickles the taste buds.
